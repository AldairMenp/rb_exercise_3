Top Video Games

Discover some of the most popular and influential video games.

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

An open-world adventure game where players explore the vast land of Hyrule, solving puzzles and battling foes to restore peace and save Princess Zelda.

Red Dead Redemption 2

Set in the American Wild West, this action-adventure game follows Arthur Morgan, an outlaw struggling to survive as the world changes around him.

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

An epic fantasy RPG that follows Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter, as he searches for his adopted daughter while navigating a war-torn world filled with magic and treachery.

Minecraft

A sandbox game that allows players to build and explore procedurally generated worlds made up of blocks. Its creativity and open-ended gameplay have made it a global phenomenon.

Cyberpunk 2077

Set in a dystopian future, this RPG lets players take on the role of V, a mercenary navigating the dark underbelly of Night City in search of immortality.
